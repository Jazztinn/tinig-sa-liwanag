# Non-native Evaluation Recording Script

Purpose: 20-clip robustness subset for non-native Hiligaynon speech.

Rules:

- Record only with speaker consent.
- Speaker must be labeled `speaker.fluency: non_native`.
- Save WAV as mono, 16 kHz, signed 16-bit PCM.
- Use IDs `nonnat_001.wav` through `nonnat_020.wav`.
- Keep subset out of headline score.
- Score only after transcript and token-language review.
