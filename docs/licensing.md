licensed under the MIT License unless otherwise stated.

## Dataset

The project-owned dataset files under `data/` are licensed under Creative
Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0).

This includes:

- annotations created by Team Hague
- benchmark rows created by Team Hague
- project-owned prediction files
- project-owned text samples

## Attribution

When using the dataset, cite:

```text
Team Hague. Sugidanon: Code-switched Hiligaynon speech and
translation benchmark scaffold. 2026. CC BY 4.0.
```

## Third-party data

Third-party resources are not relicensed by this repository. If external data is
downloaded or ingested, its original license still applies.

Examples:

- Kaikki/Wiktionary-derived dictionary data may carry Wiktionary/CC BY-SA
  obligations.
- Glosbe is documented as a manual reference source, not as a bulk-redistributed
  dataset.
- Any external audio corpus must keep its original license and attribution.

## Speaker consent

Future speech recordings must have informed consent before publication. Do not
publish personally identifying metadata beyond coarse fields such as region,
age band, and gender.
